99dps - a live DPS meter for EverQuest (Project 1999)
=====================================================

Quick start - about 5 minutes:


1) TURN ON LOGGING IN EVERQUEST.
   In the game, type this and press Enter:

        /log on

   Do this once for every character you play. (Without logging on,
   the meter has nothing to read and will look empty.)


2) RUN THE PROGRAM.
   Double-click  99dps.exe


3) IF WINDOWS SHOWS A BLUE WARNING:  "Windows protected your PC"

   This is normal for small free tools - it just means the file
   isn't from a big paid company. It is safe.

        - Click  "More info"
        - Click  "Run anyway"

   You only have to do this the FIRST time.

   (If your antivirus pops up and removes the file, tell it to
    "allow" or "restore" - same reason: it's an unknown small tool.)


4) PICK YOUR EVERQUEST FOLDER.
   The first time, 99dps tries to find your EverQuest folder and
   asks you to confirm it - or shows a window to pick it. Choose
   your EverQuest folder. It remembers your choice from then on.


5) PLAY.
   The meter updates live while you fight. Leave it open on a second
   monitor if you have one.


Tips
----
- It looks best in "Windows Terminal" (search for it in the Start
  menu, then drag 99dps.exe into it). Double-clicking works too.
- For the cleanest, tightest look, set a compact monospace font like
  "Iosevka Term" (free: github.com/be5invis/Iosevka/releases). In
  Windows Terminal:  Settings -> your profile -> Appearance ->
  Font face -> Iosevka Term. Any monospace font works; this one just
  fits more on screen while staying readable.
- Press the 'a' key to toggle spoken audio cues for low buffs.
- To quit: press Ctrl+C, or just close the window.

Have fun!
