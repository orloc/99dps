# P99 Zone Spawn Timers

Default zone-wide respawn timers for Project 1999, recorded from
<https://wiki.project1999.com/Zone_Spawn_Timers> (fetched 2026-06-07).

The wiki's own caveat: **"Note that many exceptions occur in each zone."** These
are the *default* zone-wide respawns; named mobs, placeholders, and special mob
types frequently differ (the known per-zone exceptions are listed inline).

## Time format

- **Two parts = `MM:SS`** (minutes:seconds). The standard EQ trash respawn is
  `6:40` = 6 min 40 sec = **400 seconds** — this anchors the format.
- **Three parts = `HH:MM:SS`** (hours:minutes:seconds), used for the long
  raid-zone repops (e.g. `8:00:00` = 8 hours).
- `MM:SS` with no number = the wiki has **no data** for that zone (incomplete).
- Time-of-day note: 1 in-game hour = 3 real minutes; in-game night is 8pm–6am
  (relevant to night/vampire spawns below).

## Timer variations / exceptions (summary)

- **Named mobs** generally have much longer timers than zone trash (e.g.
  Highpass Hold named 22:00 vs. 5:00 trash).
- **Placeholders** spawn on their own schedule (e.g. Splitpaw's Ishva Mal line
  at 28:00).
- **Time-of-day spawns**: Mistmoore vampires respawn at 6AM/8PM and reset the
  zone timer; night spawns are tied to in-game 8pm–6am.
- **Per-mob-type sub-timers** within a single zone: High Keep, Qeynos
  Catacombs, Wakening Land, Butcherblock, Misty Thicket, Erudin (see inline).
- **Incomplete on the wiki** (no value recorded): Kelethin, Dagnor's Cauldron,
  Emerald Jungle, Veeshan's Peak, Western Wastes.

> Source caveat: these are default zone-wide values transcribed from the P99
> wiki; individual named/raid spawn windows and per-camp variance are not
> captured here and should be confirmed per target.
